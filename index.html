Pig Dice

    how to play
  • Object of the game: to reach or exceed 100 points
  • Play begins with a roll of a die
  • Anytime a 1 is rolled, the current turn ends and no points are added to the current player's total score
  • On roll of anything besides 1, player elects to "hold" or roll again.
  • On "roll again" all the previous rolls are summed as a turn's score
  • "Hold" adds the current turn's sum to the player's total score
